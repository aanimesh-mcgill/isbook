**Assessment: Evaluating Data Systems in the Age of AI**

This assessment challenges you to apply the concepts from this chapter to real-world scenarios. Reflect on your understanding of enterprise data systems, their roles in supporting AI, and the strategic decisions necessary for successful implementation.

### Question 1: Identifying Data Silo Issues

**Scenario:** Meridian Retail Group is facing challenges with its AI-driven recommendation system due to data silos. As the data architect, your task is to identify the potential impacts of these silos on AI performance.

- What are two significant consequences of data silos on AI effectiveness? Provide specific examples based on Meridian's operations.

### Question 2: Evaluating Database Choices

**Scenario:** The VP of Data, Priya Sharma, is considering expanding Meridian's database infrastructure. The options are to either enhance the existing relational databases or integrate a NoSQL solution.

- Given the company's need for scalability and flexibility, which database type would you recommend? Justify your decision with at least two reasons.

### Question 3: Data Quality and AI Outcomes

**Scenario:** A recent audit at Meridian revealed inconsistencies in customer feedback data stored in their MongoDB system, affecting AI predictions.

- Describe two strategies to improve data quality and ensure reliable AI outputs. How do these strategies address specific issues at Meridian?

### Question 4: Technology Integration

**Scenario:** Meridian plans to integrate a new data warehouse to centralize its historical sales data. The leadership team is debating between Snowflake and Google BigQuery.

- Compare the two platforms in terms of scalability, ease of integration, and cost-effectiveness. Which would you recommend and why?

### Question 5: Assessing Data Governance Needs

**Scenario:** As Meridian expands its use of AI, data governance becomes increasingly critical. Priya Sharma is tasked with developing a governance framework.

- List three key components of an effective data governance strategy. How do these components help mitigate risks associated with AI deployments?

### Question 6: Real-Time Data Processing

**Scenario:** To enhance customer personalization, Meridian requires real-time data processing capabilities.

- Suggest a suitable technology to implement real-time data ingestion and processing. Explain why this choice aligns with Meridian's business objectives.

### Question 7: Future Challenges in Data and AI

**Scenario:** Looking ahead, Meridian aims to further integrate AI with its data systems. Identify two potential challenges they might face and propose solutions to address them effectively.

---

By engaging with these questions, you can demonstrate your ability to analyze and apply data strategies effectively in AI-driven enterprises. Reflect on your answers and consider how they align with the strategic goals of organizations like Meridian Retail Group.
