**Think First: Can AI Replace Databases?**

Before diving into the technicalities of databases and AI, let's challenge your current understanding. This exercise is designed to spark curiosity and prepare you for the concepts ahead. Answer the following questions honestly, based on your existing knowledge and intuition. Remember, there are no immediate right or wrong answers. The goal is to stimulate critical thinking.

---

**Scenario 1: The AI Misconception**

Imagine you run a mid-sized e-commerce business. Your team has implemented a cutting-edge AI system to manage customer interactions and sales analytics. A senior executive proposes discontinuing your database systems, claiming that the AI can handle all data operations. 

Choose one:

□ Yes, the AI can fully replace the databases.

□ No, databases are still necessary.

□ It depends on the context.

**Write two reasons for your choice before proceeding.**

---

**Scenario 2: Real-Time Inventory Management**

Think about a logistics company that manages thousands of shipments daily. Where would the real-time data about the number of items in a warehouse likely be stored?

Choose one:

□ AI system

□ Integrated database

□ Spreadsheet

□ Manual logs

**Explain your reasoning.**
