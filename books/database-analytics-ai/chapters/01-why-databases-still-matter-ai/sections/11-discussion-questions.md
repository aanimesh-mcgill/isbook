## Discussion Questions

To deepen your understanding of why databases continue to be critical in the age of AI, consider the following discussion questions. These are designed to encourage critical thinking about the appropriate use of database technologies in modern analytics environments.

### 1. The Role of Databases in AI Systems

:::exercise
**Question:** In a world increasingly dominated by AI technologies, what are the key reasons that databases still play a crucial role? Consider the types of data AI systems require and how databases support these needs.  
**Hint:** Think about structured vs. unstructured data and the need for reliable data storage and retrieval systems.
:::

### 2. Choice of Database Technology

:::manager
**Question:** Given a scenario where a company like Netflix needs to manage both real-time recommendations and historical viewing statistics, which databases would you recommend and why?  
**Hint:** Consider databases mentioned in the opening case, such as DynamoDB and Redshift, and their specific strengths.
:::

### 3. CAP Theorem Application

:::research
**Question:** How does the CAP theorem influence the choice of database systems in distributed architectures? Provide an example of a trade-off decision a company like Amazon might face.  
**Hint:** Discuss consistency, availability, and partition tolerance in the context of real-time vs. batch processing systems.
:::

### 4. Architectural Design: Integrating AI with Databases

:::case
**Question:** Design an architecture for an AI-driven analytics platform that needs to handle both structured data (e.g., sales transactions) and unstructured data (e.g., customer reviews). What types of databases would you integrate, and how would they interact?  
**Hint:** Consider using a combination of relational databases, data lakes, and vector databases for different data types.
:::

### 5. AI and Vector Databases

:::ai
**Question:** Why might a company like OpenAI choose to use vector databases for retrieval-augmented generation (RAG)? Discuss the advantages and potential challenges of vector databases in AI applications.  
**Hint:** Think about how vector databases manage unstructured data and the role they play in improving AI model performance.
:::

These questions aim to equip you with the ability to discern which database technology fits which problem, an essential skill in the analytics profession.
