## Discussion Questions

In this section, we aim to provoke thought and discussion around the concepts of data types and database systems in the context of analytics and AI. The questions below are designed to encourage critical thinking and practical understanding.

### Question 1: Choosing the Right Database for the Right Data

Which type of database would you recommend for storing and querying transactional data from a bank, and why? Consider the ACID properties and the need for real-time analytics. How would your choice change if the data were semi-structured?

### Question 2: Database Architecture Design

Imagine you are tasked with designing a data architecture for a new social media platform similar to Instagram. The platform needs to handle images, text, and real-time user interactions. What are the key considerations in choosing the database systems for this platform? Discuss the role of distributed databases and the CAP theorem in your design.

### Question 3: Integration of AI and Databases

Discuss the potential benefits and challenges of integrating AI technologies, like those used by OpenAI, with traditional SQL databases. How would the integration of a vector database enhance the capabilities of an AI-driven analytics pipeline?

### Question 4: Trade-offs in Data Consistency and Availability

Consider a global e-commerce platform like Amazon. Discuss the trade-offs between consistency and availability in their database systems. How does the CAP theorem guide the design of their distributed databases to ensure both reliability and scalability?

### Question 5: Handling Unstructured Data in Analytics

Given a company like Netflix, which deals with a large volume of unstructured data such as videos and user reviews, what database technologies would you recommend? How do these technologies facilitate the creation of AI models that personalize user experiences?

Reflect on your current or past projects. Which database systems did you use, and what were the driving factors behind those choices? Discuss how these decisions impacted the success of the project.

These questions are designed to stimulate critical thinking and deep understanding of how different database systems can be leveraged to address specific analytics challenges in the AI era.
