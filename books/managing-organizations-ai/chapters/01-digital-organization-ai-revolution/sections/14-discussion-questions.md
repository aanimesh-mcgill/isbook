# Discussion Questions

## Questions for Classroom Discussion

1. **How are digital technologies reshaping traditional business models?**
   - Consider the role of AI in transforming value creation processes. Discuss specific examples where AI has either disrupted or enhanced traditional ways of doing business. Why should managers be particularly attentive to these changes?

2. **What ethical considerations should managers keep in mind when implementing AI in decision-making processes?**
   - Explore the ethical dilemmas associated with AI, such as bias, transparency, and accountability. How can managers ensure that their use of AI is both ethical and effective? Cite recent research on AI ethics to support your discussion.

3. **In what ways can AI and digital technologies lead to changes in organizational structure?**
   - Analyze how AI might influence the hierarchy, communication, and decision-making processes within an organization. Discuss if and how these changes can lead to more agile and responsive organizations. Are there any research findings that suggest best practices for managers navigating these changes?

4. **What are the potential risks and rewards of integrating AI into existing business processes?**
   - Evaluate both the potential benefits and challenges of AI integration. Why is it crucial for managers to balance innovation with risk management? Provide examples from current business practices where AI integration has led to significant outcomes, positive or negative.

5. **How can managers prepare their workforce for the transition to a more AI-driven organization?**
   - Discuss strategies for upskilling and reskilling employees to work effectively alongside AI technologies. What role does organizational culture play in this transition, and how can managers foster a culture that embraces digital transformation? Reference studies that highlight successful workforce adaptation strategies.

By engaging with these questions, students will delve into the complex interplay between digital technologies, AI, and organizational management, fostering a deeper understanding of how to navigate and lead in the modern business landscape.
